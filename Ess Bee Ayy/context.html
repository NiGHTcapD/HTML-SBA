Do you want some music?
I'm fond of tracker music, and music by tracker musicians. A lot of them come across as songs, with a defined start and end point, but I like to do coding things while listening to "extended" music. These are most often found in video games, and wouldn't you know it, tracker musicians are often video game composers. My go-to pick is the Bejeweled series, whose music is composed by Skaven252 aka Peter Hajba. He's classic in the demoscene and still active on Soundcloud.
